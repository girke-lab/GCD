TU Transcriptional Unit: includes 5'/3' UTRs, exons and introns.
CDS Protein encoding nucleotide sequence.
Protein Amino acid sequence encoded by CDS.
5' UTR Transcribed non-coding sequence upstream of CDS.
3' UTR Transcribed non-coding sequence downstream of CDS.
Intergenic All sequence data up to 6000 base pairs upstream of the gene.
Promoter 3000 The intergenic sequence limited to 3000; no overlap with TU.
